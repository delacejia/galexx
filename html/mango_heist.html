THE GREAT MANGO HEIST OF '22

The year is 2022. The state of agriculture is literally unchained. Within weeks of its initial identification, the Severe Hella Acute Fungus Infecting Mangos (shafim) has ravaged the world's entire Mango population. The only mangos left in the world reside in the New Jersey maximum security mango complex, the US’s top mango regeneration facility. Scientists hope to re-engineer the common mango’s DNA to develop a natural resistance against shafim. However, you want a snack and cannot wait for such scientific tomfoolery and are desperate to thwart this effort to feast on the world’s last mangos and hence orchestrate the greatest heist this world has ever seen.
